About Me

I am a Game Developer & Designer focused on building structured gameplay systems and meaningful interactive experiences using Unity and C#.

My work spans 3D action prototypes, 2D strategy systems, time-based mechanics, and turn-based game design. I enjoy designing clear gameplay loops, balancing difficulty progression, and implementing scalable architecture that supports long-term feature expansion.

I approach development with both technical precision and design thinking — ensuring that mechanics are not only functional, but intentional, responsive, and engaging for players.

I am particularly interested in gameplay architecture, AI behavior systems, multiplayer mechanics, and system balancing. My goal is to contribute to teams that value thoughtful design, clean implementation, and polished player experiences.
