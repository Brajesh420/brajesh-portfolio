Battle Of Towers

2D Strategic Defense System

Overview

Battle Of Towers is a 2D tower defense strategy prototype built in Unity, focused on wave progression systems, resource balancing, and strategic player decision-making. The project explores structured difficulty scaling and tactical gameplay under increasing pressure.

▶ Watch Gameplay on YouTube

Core Gameplay Loop

Build Towers → Defend Against Waves → Earn Resources → Upgrade → Survive Next Wave

The loop encourages strategic placement, upgrade timing, and adaptive decision-making based on enemy types and wave difficulty.

Design Decisions

Towers were balanced around cost-to-effectiveness ratio to prevent dominant strategies. Enemy waves were structured to introduce new challenges gradually, encouraging player adaptation rather than overwhelming difficulty spikes.

Technical Implementation

• Implemented dynamic wave management system • Designed enemy path-following logic • Built tower targeting and attack systems • Structured resource economy framework • Developed scalable upgrade architecture

Challenges & Solutions

Balancing enemy progression required multiple iteration cycles. Early versions caused sudden difficulty spikes. Adjustments to spawn intervals, health scaling, and reward values created a smoother progression curve.

What I Learned

This project strengthened my understanding of system balancing, player progression design, and scalable gameplay architecture in strategy-based mechanics.
